([C++](Cpp.htm)) [GetRegexMatches](CppGetRegexMatches.htm)
==========================================================

 

![STL](PicStl.png)![Qt
Creator](PicQtCreator.png)![Lubuntu](PicLubuntu.png)

 

[GetRegexMatches](CppGetRegexMatches.htm) is a [Regex](CppRegex.htm)
[code snippet](CppCodeSnippets.htm) to obtain all
[std::strings](CppStdString.htm) in a [std::string](CppStdString.htm)
that satisfy a regular expression.

 

[GetRegexMatches](CppGetRegexMatches.htm) is demonstrated in the
[tool](Tools.htm) [RegexTester](ToolRegexTester.htm).

    'GetRegexMatches' (zip)](CppGetRegexMatches.zip)
