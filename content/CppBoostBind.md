===========================================

 

[boost::bind](CppBind.md) is a generalization of the
[std::bind1st](CppBind1st.md) and [std::bind2nd](CppBind2nd.md)
[binders](CppBinder.md).

 

 

 

 

 

[Advice](CppAdvice.md)
-----------------------

 

-   Prefer [Lambda expressions](CppLambdaExpression.md) over
    [binders](CppBinder.md) \[1\]
